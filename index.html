Get ready for Hackatly-24
Hackatly-24 is a two day hackathon conducted by the IEDC unit of Thalassery engineering college. It's a two day event in which many students take part.

Themes for Hackatly-24

Hackatly-24 is taking a different route from previous hackathons, this time. We are conducting hackatly in three phases. The first phase is an elimination phase in which some of the best ideas are selected among the submitted ones.

Phase I

The first phase is an elimination phase in which some of the best ideas are selected among the submitted ones. Multiple teams from a college can apply for the hackathon. From which one team is selected for the second and third phase.

Phase II

The second phase is the preparation phase where students are required to prepare their ideas in the provided one month period. Make the best out of your ideas. It could worth a fortune.

Phase III

Phase three is showtime. Show case your ideas in front of professionals to win exciting prizes and new connections. We expect you to keep growing your ideas even after this competition.

About Us

Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC)

 

A flagship initiative of Kerala Startup Mission to promote innovation and entrepreneurship among the student and academic fraternity in the educational institutions in the State of Kerala and is considered an umbrella program that would play an instrumental role in fostering innovation culture in Academic institutions.IEDC CETLY formed in the year 2015, is a centre supported by the Kerala Startup mission encouraging students to think creatively and generate innovative ideas. The centre aims to nurture a mindset to think creatively and innovate to develop useful product. IEDC CE-TLY serves as a helping hand among the students within the campus to figure out the best ideas and provide them with all the requirements thereby resulting to find the best upcoming entrepreneurs.

Our Legacy

IEDC unit of Thalassery Engineering college has been conducting hackatly for past few years. We have been successful in encouraging great ideas from great minds.
