Themes for Hackatly-24

Hackatly-24 is taking a different route from previous hackathons, this time. We are conducting hackatly in three phases.

Phase I

The first phase is an ideation phase where ideas should be from the given set of themes . From which the best ideas are selected for phase 2 . Multiple teams from a college can participate for the hackathon.

Phase II

The second phase is execution phase where participants will be given a set of problem statements from the same themes to choose from . A time period of one month will be provided for preparation and execution. Only 20 teams will be selected for phase 3.

Phase III

The third phase is to showcase your ideas in-front of professionals to win exciting prizes and new connection. We expect you to keep growing your ideas even after this competition.

About Us

 

A flagship initiative of Kerala Startup Mission to promote innovation and entrepreneurship among the student and academic fraternity in the educational institutions in the State of Kerala and is considered an umbrella program that would play an instrumental role in fostering innovation culture in Academic institutions.IEDC CETLY formed in the year 2015, is a centre supported by the Kerala Startup mission encouraging students to think creatively and generate innovative ideas. The centre aims to nurture a mindset to think creatively and innovate to develop useful product. IEDC CE-TLY serves as a helping hand among the students within the campus to figure out the best ideas and provide them with all the requirements thereby resulting to find the best upcoming entrepreneurs.

Our Legacy

IEDC unit of Thalassery Engineering college has been conducting hackatly for past few years. We have been successful in encouraging great ideas from great minds.
