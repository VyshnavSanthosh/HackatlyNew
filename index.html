Get ready for Hackatly-24

Hackatly 24 is a national hackathon conducted by the IEDC unit of thalassery engineering college.We are taking a different route from previous hackathons this time.We are expecting many teams to participate in the event this year

Hackatly-24 Phases

Hackatly-24 is taking a different route from previous hackathons, this time. We are conducting hackatly in three phases.

Phase I

The first phase is an ideation phase where ideas should be from the given set of themes . From which the best ideas are selected for phase 2 . Multiple teams from a college can participate for the hackathon.

Phase II

The second phase is execution phase where participants from the previous phase will be given a time period of one month for preparation and execution. Only 20 teams will be selected for phase 3.

Phase III

The third phase is to showcase your ideas in-front of professionals to win exciting prizes and new connection. We expect you to keep growing your ideas even after this competition.

About Us

 

A flagship initiative of Kerala Startup Mission to promote innovation and entrepreneurship among the student and academic fraternity in the educational institutions in the State of Kerala and is considered an umbrella program that would play an instrumental role in fostering innovation culture in Academic institutions.IEDC CETLY formed in the year 2015, is a centre supported by the Kerala Startup mission encouraging students to think creatively and generate innovative ideas. The centre aims to nurture a mindset to think creatively and innovate to develop useful product. IEDC CE-TLY serves as a helping hand among the students within the campus to figure out the best ideas and provide them with all the requirements thereby resulting to find the best upcoming entrepreneurs.

Our Legacy

IEDC unit of Thalassery Engineering college has been conducting hackatly for past few years. We have been successful in encouraging great ideas from great minds.

Legacy of IEDC

Inception (2022): First hackathon launched by IEDC Thalassery, setting a strong foundation for innovation and collaboration.

Growth (2023):Expanded participation, more complex challenges, and increased industry involvement; solidified the event's reputation.

Hackatly 24 (2024):Launching the biggest edition yet, with broader themes, a larger participant base, and enhanced industry partnerships.

Legacy:A consistent track record of fostering creativity, teamwork, and technological advancement since 2022.

Our History

Since 2022, IEDC Thalassery has successfully hosted annual hackathons, establishing a strong legacy of innovation and collaboration. Each year, the event has grown in scale, attracting more participants and industry support. Now, as they launch Hackatly 24 in 2024, the tradition continues with even greater ambition, promising to push the boundaries of creativity and technology further than ever before.

Previous Hackathon

"Hackatly 23" was a 2-day state-level hackathon held on May 4-5, 2023, with the theme "Innovation for Easy Life". Out of 25 participating teams, the winners were:

Vrishti (College of Engineering Trivandrum) - Developed adult incontinence products and baby diapers.

Mark-10 (Model Engineering College Thrikkakara) - Created a cultural system using AI in a mobile application (1st runner-up).

Uncertainty (Amal Jyothi College of Engineering) - Designed a local clinic doctor booking system (2nd runner-up).
